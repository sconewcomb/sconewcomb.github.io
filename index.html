About

I was born and raised in Richmond, Virginia and have been interested in the arts and technology my whole life. I enjoy drawing, playing my guitar, gaming and the occasional home improvement project. These interests contribute to my creativity and problem solving ability, which are things that drove me into the technology world.

I attended ECPI and graduated with a B.S. in Information Science Network Security. With a solid background in networking, I then expanded my web development skills. Most recently my path has led me to email design and development. Currently, I'm working as a Front End Developer at Capital One on the Email Messaging team. With these newly discovered skills I have been able to build upon my generalist skillset. I want to continue incorporating this knowledge to learn and understand new technologies for producing a better user experience for the client.
